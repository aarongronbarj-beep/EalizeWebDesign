About

Why we built
Ealize Sites.

There are millions of brilliant small businesses that can't be found online — not because they don't deserve a website, but because nobody made it affordable and easy enough.

The problem we kept seeing

We kept running into the same situation. A great little restaurant, a skilled plumber, a talented hair stylist — all losing customers to worse businesses that just happened to have a website.

The owners knew they needed one. But every agency they called quoted them €3,000 minimum, then booked them in for "discovery meetings" that stretched on for weeks. So they put it off. And put it off. And kept losing business to competitors who were easier to find online.

We built Ealize Sites to fix that. Professional websites, built fast, at a price that makes sense for a business earning €5,000 a month — not €50,000.

We're not trying to be everything to everyone. We do one thing: get small local businesses online, with a site they're proud of, in two to three weeks. That's it.

What we actually deliver

We don't use templates dressed up as custom. We don't outsource to another country and mark it up. We design each site around the specific business — its tone, its customers, its goals.

Every site we build is mobile-first, fast-loading, and set up properly for local search. Because what's the point of a beautiful website that Google can't find?

And when it's done — you own it completely. No lock-in, no monthly platform fees to us, no holding your site hostage.

Our mission

Too many good local businesses fail not because of their product or service — but because nobody could find them. Every small business deserves a professional online presence, regardless of how big their marketing budget is.

Honest disclaimer

What we're not going to do

We won't sell you features you don't need. If the Basic plan is right for you, we'll tell you — we'd rather build 10 honest Basic projects than upsell one client into something they don't use.

We won't overpromise on SEO. A good site helps — a lot. But anyone who guarantees page 1 within 30 days is lying. We set up your foundations right and the rankings follow.

We won't drag out the process with unnecessary meetings. One call to start, one preview to review — that's it. Your time is better spent on your business.

We won't lock you in. Your website belongs to you. We help you set up your own hosting, and you own every file. If you ever want to move on, you can — no questions, no fees.
