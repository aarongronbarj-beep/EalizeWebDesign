Common questions

Are there monthly fees?

No. You pay once for the build. Hosting is separate — we'll recommend a reliable host that costs €5–15/month, which you pay directly. We don't take a cut.

What if I want changes after launch?

Small tweaks are on us for the first 30 days. After that, we offer a simple hourly rate for any updates you need.

Do I own my website?

Yes. 100%. We build it, you own it. You can take it anywhere, host it anywhere, and we'll never hold it hostage.

I don't have any photos or content — is that a problem?

Not at all. We'll write the copy for you based on a short briefing call. For photos, we can use free professional stock images that match your business until you have your own.

How do I pay?

50% upfront to start, 50% when you approve the final design. Bank transfer or card — whatever is easier for you.
